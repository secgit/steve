About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. I'm a cybersecurity professional and CISSP-certified expert with extensive experience in identity and access management, SIEM solutions, and industrial control system security.

With a Master's degree in Computing Security from Rochester Institute of Technology and years of hands-on experience at Air Products and Chemicals, I specialize in developing security automation solutions and protecting critical infrastructure. When I'm not securing systems, you'll find me at the piano, bringing classical pieces to life.
